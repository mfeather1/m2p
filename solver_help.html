Cube Solver Help

To use the solver press the Random button and after a scrambled cube appears press Solve. When the solution appears, press the Play button (right arrow) on the control bar under the cube.

To enter a cube configuration, press Clear and then click on a color in the palette (six squares above layout) and then click on squares in the layout to color the cube.


Custom Cube Display

The following AnimCubeJS URL parameters can be passed to the solver to customize the cube display (click on any link to see documentation).


align bgcolor borderwidth butbgcolor
buttonheight clickprogress counter cubecolor
doublespeed edit fonttype hint
hintborder hinthoriz hintvert metric
movetext movetextspace perspective position
repeat scale slidercolor snap
speed textsize troughcolor yz
